About the Man

Born to Lead,
Built to Win

Mahendra Singh Dhoni was born on 7 July 1981 in Ranchi, Jharkhand. A former ticket collector who turned into India's most successful captain, Dhoni is celebrated for his ice-cold composure, lightning wicket-keeping, and match-winning finishes.

Under his captaincy, India won the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy — making him the only captain in history to win all three major ICC trophies.
